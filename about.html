Our Story

ScaleMako was founded by two graduates — one in Data Science, one in Computer Science — who spent years working at the intersection of machine learning and business operations. We saw firsthand how much revenue businesses lost simply because they couldn't answer every call or respond to every inquiry fast enough.

Our background in data-driven systems and AI model deployment gave us a clear view of what was possible. The tools existed — they just weren't being packaged in a way that everyday business owners could use and afford.

So we built ScaleMako: a focused AI service company that gives local and growing businesses the same lead-capture and automation capabilities that enterprise companies pay tens of thousands for — at a price that makes sense.
