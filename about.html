Our Story

ScaleMako was born from a simple idea: what if we could help businesses grow while learning and building something meaningful ourselves?

We're two college students who saw a gap in the market. Small businesses need AI automation solutions, but they're often too expensive or too complex. At the same time, we wanted to gain real world experience building products that actually help people.

So we decided to combine both goals. We're building ScaleMako to help others grow their businesses while we gain valuable experience and build our own.

Our Mission

Our mission is threefold:

  • Help businesses scale. We provide affordable, accessible AI automation solutions that help small businesses compete with larger companies.
  • Learn and grow. As college students, we're using this project to gain hands on experience in business, technology, and customer service.
  • Build something real. We're not doing this for a grade. We're building a real business that solves real problems for real people.
